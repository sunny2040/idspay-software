Providing Safe Water

Providing access to safe water and sanitation is crucial for ensuring public health and well-being. It prevents the spread of waterborne diseases, promotes hygiene, and enhances quality of life by safeguarding individuals and communities from harmful contaminants. Access to clean water and proper sanitation facilities supports overall health, development, and productivity.

Old Age Homes

A roof over their heads is a critical need of the elder who are destitute, sick and abandoned by family and those uprooted by disasters. IDSPay care has established model recreational centre for the senior citizens and aged in places such as Delhi. IDSPay care supports 285 old age citizens of India. Replacing it with an attitude of confidence, fostering respect for them and encouraging fortitude in them.
