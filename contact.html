Prakati Kanchan Seva Foundation working in cooperation with various stakeholders to bring about difference in the lives of underprivileged, deprived women, children and their families and communities is what Prakati Kanchan Seva Foundation is all about. Prakati Kanchan Seva Foundation care is a registered voluntary NGO which has been working for the empowerment of marginalized sections of society since 2002. Prakati Kanchan Seva Foundation Care was started with a view to bring smile in the lives of such people.
